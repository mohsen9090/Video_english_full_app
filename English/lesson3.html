Lesson 3: Daily Routines

Lesson Objective:

In this lesson, you will learn how to describe your daily routines and activities.

Key Vocabulary:

  • Wake up
  • Brush teeth
  • Have breakfast
  • Go to work/school
  • Come home
  • Go to bed

Sample Dialogues:

Dialogue 1:

Person A: What time do you usually wake up?

Person B: I usually wake up at 6:30 AM.

Dialogue 2:

Person A: What do you do after you wake up?

Person B: I brush my teeth and have breakfast.

Dialogue 3:

Person A: How do you get to work?

Person B: I usually take the bus.

Dialogue 4:

Person A: What time do you come home?

Person B: I come home around 5 PM.

Dialogue 5:

Person A: What do you do in the evening?

Person B: I usually relax and watch TV before going to bed.
