Lesson 4: What Time Do You Wake Up?

Lesson Objective:

In this lesson, you will learn how to talk about your daily routine, specifically focusing on waking up and morning activities.

Key Vocabulary:

  • Wake up
  • Alarm clock
  • Morning
  • Breakfast

Sample Dialogues:

Dialogue 1:

Person A: What time do you wake up?

Person B: I usually wake up at 7 AM.

Person A: Do you use an alarm clock?

Person B: Yes, I set my alarm for 6:45 AM.

Dialogue 2:

Person A: What do you do after you wake up?

Person B: I usually take a shower and then have breakfast.

Dialogue 3:

Person A: What do you like to eat for breakfast?

Person B: I love oatmeal and fruit.

Dialogue 4:

Person A: How long does it take you to get ready in the morning?

Person B: About 30 minutes.

Dialogue 5:

Person A: Do you have a morning routine?

Person B: Yes, I meditate for 10 minutes every morning.
