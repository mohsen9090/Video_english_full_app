Lesson 7: Talking About Hobbies

Lesson Objective:

In this lesson, you will learn expressions and phrases related to hobbies and activities you enjoy doing in your free time.

Key Vocabulary:

  • Read books
  • Play sports
  • Watch movies
  • Travel
  • Sing
  • Paint

Sample Dialogues:

Dialogue 1:

Person A: What do you do in your free time?

Person B: I like to read books and play sports.

Dialogue 2:

Person A: Do you enjoy watching movies?

Person B: Yes, I love watching movies, especially comedies.

Dialogue 3:

Person A: What kind of sports do you play?

Person B: I play soccer and basketball.

Dialogue 4:

Person A: Do you have any hobbies?

Person B: Yes, I enjoy painting and singing.

Dialogue 5:

Person A: Where do you like to travel?

Person B: I love traveling to the mountains and the beach.
