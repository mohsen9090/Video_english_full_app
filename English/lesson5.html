Lesson 5: Talking About the Future

Lesson Objective:

In this lesson, you will learn how to talk about future events and plans in English. You will also learn about different ways to express the future tense.

Key Vocabulary:

  • Will
  • Going to
  • Future
  • Plan

Sample Dialogues:

Dialogue 1:

Person A: What are you going to do this weekend?

Person B: I am going to visit my grandparents.

Dialogue 2:

Person A: Are you going to watch the game tomorrow?

Person B: Yes, I will definitely watch it!

Dialogue 3:

Person A: What will you do if it rains?

Person B: If it rains, I will stay home and read a book.

Dialogue 4:

Person A: Do you have any plans for the summer?

Person B: Yes, I am going to travel to Europe.

Dialogue 5:

Person A: What are your goals for next year?

Person B: I will focus on my studies and improve my grades.
