Lesson 11: Talking About Work

Lesson Objective:

In this lesson, you will learn how to talk about your job, responsibilities, and work-related topics.

Key Vocabulary:

  • Job
  • Employee
  • Employer
  • Responsibilities
  • Office
  • Meeting
  • Project
  • Deadline
  • Promotion
  • Salary

Sample Dialogues:

Dialogue 1:

Person A: What do you do for work?

Person B: I work as a software developer at a tech company.

Person A: That sounds interesting! What are your responsibilities?

Person B: My main responsibilities include coding, testing, and maintaining applications.

Dialogue 2:

Person A: Do you work in an office or remotely?

Person B: I work remotely from home, but we have weekly meetings online.

Dialogue 3:

Person A: How do you handle deadlines?

Person B: I plan my tasks carefully and make sure to finish everything ahead of time to avoid stress.

Dialogue 4:

Person A: Do you like your job?

Person B: Yes, I enjoy solving problems and creating solutions, but sometimes the workload can be overwhelming.

Dialogue 5:

Person A: How often do you get promotions?

Person B: Promotions are not very frequent, but I get regular feedback and opportunities for growth.
