Lesson 1: Introduction to English Conversation

Lesson Objective:

In this lesson, we will cover basic greetings and how to introduce yourself in English. The focus is on conversational phrases that are useful in daily situations.

Key Vocabulary:

  • Hello
  • How are you?
  • My name is...
  • Nice to meet you

Sample Dialogues:

Dialogue 1:

Person A: Hello, how are you?

Person B: I'm good, thank you! And you?

Person A: I'm doing well, thanks for asking. My name is John. What is your name?

Person B: My name is Sarah. Nice to meet you!

Person A: Nice to meet you too, Sarah!
